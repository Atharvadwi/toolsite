Delivering Quality Castings for Every Industry.

Established in 1987, Tulsi Precision Product LLP began with a clear vision — to manufacture high-quality casting components that meet the evolving demands of the automotive and engineering industries.

A systematic, quality-driven process ensuring every casting component meets the highest standards.
